🌠 About Space

🌌 The Universe

The universe is estimated to be 13.8 billion years old and consists of billions of galaxies, each with millions of stars and planets.

🪐 The Solar System

Our solar system has eight planets, including Earth, orbiting the Sun. Each planet has unique characteristics and moons.

💫 Black Holes

Black holes are regions of space with gravitational fields so strong that not even light can escape. The first image of a black hole was captured in 2019.
